New MacDonald

The finished product: http://newmacdonald.onlyorganic.org

Partners: Humanaut, Redwood

Client: Only Organic

Technology stack: PHP, MySQL, AWS Elastic Beanstalk, ScrollMagic & GSAP, Bootstrap, Bootstrap Validator, jQuery & jQuery UI

"Animation? Sure we can do webpage animation." We almost bit off more than we could chew with this project. The real challenge was the super tight timeline coupled with the need for lots of back-and-forth with the design team at Humanaut. Luckily the ScrollMagic scrolling animation Javascript library was just ready and waiting to save our butts. In just a couple days Southerneer was able to turn around a working demo of the animation functionality and in just a couple more churn out a game-ready and unique user experience for the client. Only Organic was really happy with the scroll-enabled animations and clean, mobile-friendly design.
